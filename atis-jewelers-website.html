Our Story

Run by the Wowk family, trusted by the neighborhood.

Ati's Jewelers has been fitting rings, restoring heirlooms, and pricing honestly out of the same Garfield Road showcase since 2000. Owner Sandra Lefchick Wowk and her staff — Cheryl, Sherri, and the rest of the team — keep every repair on-site instead of shipping it out to a stranger.

Earlier this year, the State of Michigan presented the shop a special tribute marking twenty-five years in business. The Wowk family will tell you the honor belongs to the customers who kept coming back.

"A Truly Wholesale Jeweler."Sandra Lefchick Wowk, Owner
